Who We Are

CHS World stands for Caribbean Heritage & Success

CHS World is a global platform dedicated to celebrating Caribbean culture, preserving its history, and showcasing the success of its people worldwide.

We exist to connect the diaspora, inspire the next generation, and build a community rooted in identity, ambition, and pride.
